What is Pointillist?


Pointillist is a graphic design tool that transforms black & white images into Prefect's distinctive pixel grid pattern.




How to Use?


Drag & Drop - Simply drag your image into the browser window to begin the transformation. Works best with square, black & white images

Adjust - Use the controls to fine-tune the pixel grid to your liking.

Export - Once satisfied, easily export your design to SVG or PNG formats.

* Works best with Chrome
